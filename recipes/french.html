How to Make a French 75

Description

A French 75 is a wonderfully refreshing cocktail that balances sweet and sour in a delightful way. Served in a champagne flute, this cocktail is sure to be a hit at every function.

Ingredients and Tools

  • Champagne Flute
  • Cocktail Shaker
  • Strainer
  • Lemon Peeler
  • Ice
  • Gin
  • Simple Syrup
  • Lemon Juice
  • Lemon
  • Sparkling Wine

Steps

  1. Pour 1 oz of gin into your cocktail strainer.
  2. Pour 1 oz of lemon juice into your cocktail strainer.
  3. Pour half an oz of simple syrup into your cocktail strainer.
  4. Fill shaker 3/4 of the way with ice, put the top on and shake vigorously for 7-8 seconds.
  5. Take the top off the shaker, place the cocktail strainer on top of the shaker and strain the cocktail into the champagne flute.
  6. Top with sparkling wine and garnish with a twist of lemon peel. Enjoy!
