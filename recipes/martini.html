How to Make a Martini

Description

A simple cocktail as old as time, this tutorial will amaze your houseguests with your deft bartending skill.

Ingredients and Tools

  • Martini Glass
  • Cocktail Shaker
  • Strainer
  • Ice
  • Dry Vermouth
  • Vodka or Gin
  • Cocktail Skewers
  • Olives

Steps

  1. Pour 0.5 oz of dry vermouth into your martini glass. Swirl the vermouth so that it touches most parts of the glass, then dump out the vermouth.
  2. Pour 2 oz of vodka or gin into your cocktail strainer.
  3. Fill the shaker up halfway with ice, put the top on and shake vigorously for 7-8 seconds.
  4. Take the top off the shaker, place the cocktail strainer on top of the shaker and strain the cocktail into the martini glass.
  5. Garnish with two olives on a skewer. Enjoy!
