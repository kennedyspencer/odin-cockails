How to Make an Old Fashioned

Description

Notorious for its strength of flavor and beloved in the 20th century, an old fashioned is a great winter cocktail for sipping by a fireplace with loved ones.

Ingredients and Tools

  • Short Rocks Glass
  • Muddler
  • An Orange
  • Paring Knife
  • Cocktail Cherries
  • Whiskey
  • Simple Syrup
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Cut a wedge from an orange and place it in the glass with a cocktail cherry. Muddle.
  2. Pour 2 oz of whiskey in the glass.
  3. Splash with simple syrup.
  4. Add ice, garnish with an orange slice and a cherry. Enjoy!
